Possessions During Games


Possession

From the bubble chart and histogram, we can see that the possession rate of Barcelona were stablely between 50% and 75% while that of Chelsea went across those lines more often.

This means Barcelona players tend to keep the ball under their feet, while Chelsea players sometimes would choose to backup and launch counterattact instead.




Attack & Defense

Barcelona and Chelsea both had high tackle success rate and pressure success rate while Barcelona had slightly higher save success rate.

On the attacking side, although Barcelona shoots more accuratly in games (higher shoot-on-target rate), the overall efficiency (shoot-to-score rates) were similar for two teams.







🏃🏃🏽    Individual Players    🏃🏿🏃🏻




Top 11 Players by Market Value



In Season 18-19, Chelsea had a more balanced distributions of players' value than that of Barcelona.

For Barca, top 11 players accounted for more than 3/4 of the total value, while the top 11 of Chelsea only equaled to around 2/3 of the total value.






Goal Contributions



Corresponding with our conclusion from the pie charts above, Messi and Hazard scored the most goals in their teams. Barcelona and Chelsea both rely on their super stars heavily.

However, Barcelona relied much more heavily on the top player than Chelsea did, with its top2 scores contributed to 2/3 of all the goals.






Goal Assists



Different from the goal scoring stats, for the goal assists, the distributions of two clubs were very similar.

But we can still see the difference between the role of Messi and Hazard in their team: Messi had to put more effort on scoring while Hazard also contributed a lot on assist his teammates.
