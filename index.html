As the two of the most prestigious teams in world football, F.C. Barcelona and Chelsea F.C. have both maintained a very high standard in recent years.

As fans of these two teams, we are fortunate to be able to present the stories behind those victories and trophies in the form of data.

In this process, we ourselves have also gained a lot: many stereotypes about these two teams were refreshed and many underlying reasons behind wins and loses were learned.

In fact, countless details on and off the field together determine the outcome of a soccer game and the championship.

The data we selected in this project is only part of it.

We hope that our project will interest you and hopefully draw your interests in learning more about soccer.
